OutdoorAdvisor

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Overview

OutdoorAdvisor is built with privacy as a default. The app helps people in Pakistan understand outdoor conditions, air quality, pollen, and travel advisories without selling personal data or using advertising identifiers.

Information We Use

  • Location is used to fetch local weather, air quality, pollen, and travel context for your area.
  • Notification preferences, thresholds, layout choices, and similar settings are stored on your device.
  • If you choose to sign in, account information is handled by Supabase authentication.
  • If you allow Health access on iOS, step count, walking/running distance, and active energy are read on device to support smart outdoor nudges.

Information We Do Not Sell

We do not sell your data. We do not use advertising identifiers such as IDFA. We do not run advertising profiles based on your activity in OutdoorAdvisor.

Third-Party Services

OutdoorAdvisor may use third-party services to provide core app features, including Apple WeatherKit, Open-Meteo, Google weather and location APIs, AQICN, Pakistan Meteorological Department public pages, National Highways and Motorway Police public advisories, Supabase, Expo, and Vercel.

These services may process the minimum request data needed to return weather, air quality, location, authentication, hosting, or notification functionality.

Health Data

Health data is used only for user-facing smart nudges inside OutdoorAdvisor. It is not sold, used for advertising, or shared for marketing. OutdoorAdvisor does not write health data.

Children

OutdoorAdvisor is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update this page and, where appropriate, the privacy text inside the app.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to privacy@outdooradvisor.app. Support questions can be sent to support@outdooradvisor.app.
